**Level:** Professional / Production-Ready  
**Focus:** Agentic AI, Multimodal Systems, and Full-Stack Integration  
**Location:** Karachi, Pakistan (2026)

---

## 🏗️ Core Tech Stack (Mandatory for All)
* **Frontend:** Next.js 14+ (App Router) or React + Tailwind CSS
* **Backend:** FastAPI (Python) for AI orchestration
* **AI Models:** Gemini 3 Flash (Vision/Text) + Whisper (Speech)
* **Orchestration:** Vercel AI SDK or Openai SDK (for Agentic workflows)
* **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth) or Pinecone (Vector DB for RAG)
* **Real-time:** WebSockets or Ably (for live feedback/tracking)

## 📋 The Top 5 Projects

### 1. AI Real-Time Video Interview Coach
**Problem:** Local graduates struggle with interview anxiety and poor non-verbal communication.  
**AI Core:** Multimodal analysis of video and audio streams.  
**Full-Stack Features:** * **Vision:** MediaPipe to track posture, eye contact, and fidgeting.  
* **Speech:** Whisper to analyze filler words (um, ah, like) and speech pace.  
* **Agentic Twist:** The AI plays the role of a "Strict HR Manager" or "Friendly Startup Founder," adapting its questioning style based on the user's performance.  
**Portfolio Value:** Demonstrates mastery of WebRTC and real-time AI feedback loops.

### 2. AI "Legal & Contract" Guardian for Freelancers
**Problem:** Freelancers often sign international contracts with hidden "Red Flags" or unfair payment terms.  
**AI Core:** Agentic RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) for legal document analysis.  
**Full-Stack Features:** * **Parsing:** Automatic extraction of terms from PDF/Word documents.  
* **Risk Engine:** Cross-references the contract against a database of fair labor practices.  
* **Action Agent:** Automatically drafts a professional counter-offer email or highlights specific clauses that need negotiation.  
**Portfolio Value:** Shows the ability to handle complex unstructured data and automated business workflows.

### 3. AI "Kirana" Inventory Agent & Smart Procurement
**Problem:** Small shop owners lose revenue due to expired stock and lack of price visibility in the wholesale market.  
**AI Core:** Computer Vision and Web-Scraping Agents.  
**Full-Stack Features:** * **Visual Audit:** Owner takes a photo of the shelf; AI updates stock counts automatically.  
* **Procurement Agent:** The AI scrapes local B2B marketplaces to find the lowest price for out-of-stock items.  
* **WhatsApp Alerts:** Low-stock notifications and "best time to buy" recommendations via Twilio.  
**Portfolio Value:** Combines Vision, CRUD, and Web Automation in one system.

### 4. AI Women’s Safety "Sentinel" (Live Risk Scoring)
**Problem:** Urban security concerns during late-night commutes.  
**AI Core:** Real-time Geospatial intelligence and Voice-Triggered Agents.  
**Full-Stack Features:** * **Dynamic Routing:** Uses Gemini to score routes based on time, lighting, and recent local incident reports.  
* **Voice-Activation:** A background listener that triggers an "Emergency SOS" if it hears specific Urdu distress keywords.  
* **Auto-Check-in:** The system calls the user via AI Voice if they deviate from their path; failure to answer alerts emergency contacts.  
**Portfolio Value:** High social impact project involving Geofencing and Voice APIs.

### 5. AI Agri-Doctor for Sindh Farmers
**Problem:** Delayed crop disease diagnosis leads to massive harvest losses.  
**AI Core:** Multimodal Vision (Gemini 3 Flash) and Weather RAG.  
**Full-Stack Features:** * **Multilingual Support:** Full interface in Urdu and Sindhi using AI-driven localization.  
* **Vision Diagnosis:** Farmers upload leaf photos; AI identifies the pest and suggests organic/affordable treatments.  
* **Climate Intelligence:** The agent checks real-time weather APIs to advise on the exact hour to apply fertilizers or harvest.  
**Portfolio Value:** Demonstrates "Edge" AI use cases and multi-language accessibility.

## 🛠️ Implementation Guidelines
1.  **Agentic Approach:** No project should just be a "Chatbot." Every project must **take an action** (e.g., send an email, update a database, trigger a call).
2.  **Tool Use:** Students must implement "Function Calling" (allowing the LLM to interact with external APIs).
3.  **Deployment:** Projects must be Dockerized and deployed on a cloud provider (Render/Railway/Hugging-Face/Vercel).
