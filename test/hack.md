# AI-Powered Problem-Solving Projects  
**Bootcamp 4.0 – Mandatory AI Edition**  
**March 2026**  
**Location: Karachi, Pakistan**  

---

The document still contains **exactly 20 production-ready AI project ideas** focused on real Pakistani problems (Karachi/Sindh-centric). Every project has a strong AI core (mandatory), is buildable in 4–6 weeks by students, and delivers high portfolio + real-world impact value.  

---

## Recommended Tech Stack (Common for All Projects)  
- **LLM**: Google Gemini 1.5 Flash / OpenAI GPT-4o mini (free tier)  
- **Vision & Speech**: Hugging Face + Gemini Vision + Whisper  
- **RAG & Forecasting**: LangChain + Chroma / Prophet  
- **Frontend**: Streamlit (fast MVP) or React + Tailwind  
- **Backend**: FastAPI / Flask  
- **Database**: Supabase / SQLite (free)  
- **Deployment**: Vercel / Render / Railway  
- **Maps & APIs**: Google Maps + free weather/AQI APIs

## 19 AI Projects for Bootcamp 4.0  

### 1. AI Flood & Disaster Preparedness Assistant (Sindh Focus)  
**Problem**: Monsoon floods cause huge losses every year due to lack of early personalized warnings.  
**AI Role**: Predicts risk for a location and generates custom evacuation plans + nearest shelter locator.  
**Key Features**: Urdu voice alerts, family sharing.  
**Tech**: Gemini + Weather API + Maps + RAG.  
**Impact**: Life-saving public service.

### 2. AI Waste Segregation & Smart Recycling Optimizer (Karachi)  
**Problem**: Extremely poor waste management with recycling rate below 10%.  
**AI Role**: Classifies waste type from a photo and suggests nearest recycling points.  
**Key Features**: Community leaderboard and carbon footprint calculator.  
**Tech**: Hugging Face Vision + Gemini + Maps.  
**Impact**: Environment and circular economy.

### 3. AI Personalized Fitness & Pakistani Diet Coach  
**Problem**: Generic diet plans ignore local Pakistani food and budget constraints.  
**AI Role**: Creates weekly meal + exercise plans based on body stats and goals.  
**Key Features**: Halal/budget filter and progress tracking.  
**Tech**: Gemini Vision (pose correction) + Nutrition RAG.  
**Impact**: Health revolution for youth.

### 4. AI Urdu Lecture Video Summarizer & Quiz Generator  
**Problem**: Students waste hours watching long educational videos.  
**AI Role**: Summarizes video in Urdu, adds timestamps and auto-generates MCQ quizzes.  
**Key Features**: Matric/FSc/MDCAT syllabus integration.  
**Tech**: Whisper + Gemini + RAG.  
**Impact**: Education equity.

### 5. AI Freelance Proposal & Bid Winner Generator  
**Problem**: Pakistani freelancers have very low proposal win rates.  
**AI Role**: Turns any job description into a personalized winning proposal.  
**Key Features**: Skill-gap analysis and Urdu/English switch.  
**Tech**: Advanced Gemini prompting + RAG.  
**Impact**: Direct income booster.

### 6. AI Blood & Plasma Emergency Matcher

**Key Features**: Urgency notifications and history tracking.  
**Tech**: Matching algorithm + Maps + Gemini.

### 7. AI Kirana Store Demand Forecaster & Inventory Manager

### 19.  AI Real-Time Video Interview Coach

**Key Features**: Confidence scoring, recording review, and improvement suggestions.  
**Tech**: Computer Vision (MediaPipe) + Whisper + Gemini feedback.  
**Impact**: Massive employability boost and perfect for placements.

2. **Week 2–3**: AI core development and prototype.  
3. **Week 4–5**: Full UI/UX, Urdu support, and testing.  
4. **Week 6**: Deployment, GitHub portfolio, and final presentation.  

**Evaluation Criteria**: AI accuracy & usefulness (65%), User Experience + Urdu support (15%), Real impact & testing (15%), Code quality (5%).
